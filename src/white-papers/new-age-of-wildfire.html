White Paper: A New Age of Wildfire

The total damage and economic loss caused by wildfires in 2018 in California amounted to $400 billion. And the impact is only growing.

A deadly combination of climate change and aging electrical infrastructure has created a new normal across the American West: a nearly-year-round fire season.

California's technology industry is rising to the challenge to detect and fight wildfires more effectively than ever before.

This white paper explores:

  • The growing impact of wildfires on California
  • The key challenges to addressing wildfires in the state
  • New technologies helping communities monitor, fight, and recover from wildfires
